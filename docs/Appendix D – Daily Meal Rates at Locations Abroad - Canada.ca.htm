Appendix D – Daily Meal Rates at Locations Abroad

Effective: January 1, 2018

Notes:

(A) The incidental expense allowance shall be paid to CRA travellers at the following rates for commercial, private non-commercial, government, and institutional accommodation:

  • 32% of the meal total when the three meal allowances are listed in the table below;
  • 40% of the meal total when only lunch and dinner meal allowances are listed in the table below;
  • 40% of actual and reasonable expenses for meals as per paragraph 3.16.4.2.

(B) Private non-commercial accommodation allowance: $50 CAD

(C) For all locations indicating an asterisk (*) for all three meals (denoting reasonable and justifiable expenses, receipts required), the incidental allowance reflects the amount published in Appendix C, under Canada & USA, paid in Canadian dollars (CAD).

(D) For the United States of America (Outside the Continental USA only), the meals and incidental allowances reflect the amounts published in Appendix C, under Canada & USA, which is paid in US dollars (USD).
